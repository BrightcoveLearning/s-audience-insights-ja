Data

Hover over the graph to display data.

  • Media name - Name of the Series or Movie
  • Store name - Name of the app store where the content was viewed
  • Number of views - Number of times a video started playing, recorded when the stream is started (does not include rewind or replay) - this is not a measure of individual viewers
  • Hours viewed - Total number of hours that the content was viewed
